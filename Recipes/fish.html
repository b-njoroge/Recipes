Spicy Fish

Description

If you are looking for home style Indian chicken recipes for beginners, check out this easy Chicken Masala recipe. This Chicken Masala goes perfectly with both roti and rice. Serve this spicy masala chicken dish with salad and raita and you are sorted for the day! The main trick which makes this chicken masala so delicious is the proper frying of spices or the masala. The Chicken Masala Recipe is indulgent dish and hence uses oil a little more than usual. Chicken gets cooked easily so it is important to be careful in this preparation or it would fall off the bones and mix with the gravy. The process of how to make chicken and slow frying works best in this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 cup chopped onion
  • salt as required
  • 2 inches cinnamon stick
  • 2 green cardamom
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 teaspoon cumin powder

Steps

  1. Wash and clean the chicken
  2. saute whole spices
  3. Add spices in frying chicken
  4. Cook for 2-4 minutes with closed lid and serve hot
